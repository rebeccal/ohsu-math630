What is Due & When?

Fall 2019

You are expected to come to each class meeting prepared, which means that you will have done the readings and DataCamp assignments that are due for that date before coming to class.

Readings

Readings will primarily be from two free, open-source, completely online textbooks:

  1. MODERN DIVE into Data with R (MD)
  2. R for Data Science (R4DS)

These books will be supplemented by blog posts, YouTube videos, and several open-access articles, including from:

  • A special PEERJ issue called “Practical Data Science for Stats”, and
  • Nature: Points of Significance

Ramping Up

The following DataCamp courses, tutorials, and readings are optional, but will help you come up to speed with the skills you’ll need to complete this class. Unless you have strong, recent experience with R, RStudio, RMarkdown and the TidyVerse, I strongly suggest you complete, or at least review, them.
